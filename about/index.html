Dr. Alyssa Ailion, PhD, ABPP-CN

Dr. Ailion provides private neuropsychological assessment (in-person in the Boston area) as well as virtual therapy and consultation services for children, adolescents, and young adults. Our evaluations are warm, collaborative, and focused on accomplishing your specific goals.

Dr. Ailion’s evaluations provide a comprehensive examination of learning, attention, executive functioning, medical/neurological factors, and emotional challenges. Findings are used to guide treatment planning, school supports (IEP/504), and accommodations when appropriate.

She has particular expertise working with complex and medical populations, including cases where neurological or health factors intersect with learning, attention, executive functioning, and emotional well-being.

Training and experience

  • Licensed clinical psychologist
  • Board-certified clinical neuropsychologist (ABPP-CN)
  • Assistant Professor of Psychology at Harvard Medical School
  • Affiliated with Boston Children’s Hospital
  • Over a decade of experience in neuropsychological assessment

What It’s Like to Work With Us

Families often come to us feeling overwhelmed by unanswered questions, school concerns, medical complexity, or uncertainty about next steps. Dr. Ailion’s approach is collaborative, thoughtful, and highly individualized. She works to understand each child or young adult quickly and deeply, while helping families feel organized, informed, and supported throughout the process.

The goal is not just to provide test scores or diagnoses. The goal is to translate complex information into a clear, practical plan that families, schools, physicians, therapists, and other providers can actually use.

Our practice

Esplanade Psychological Services is a boutique practice focused on timely access to care, rapid scheduling when possible, and a clear understanding of your (or your child’s) specific needs. Our goal is to deliver thorough, individualized services—and translate findings into practical recommendations that families, schools, and providers can use.

Practice locations / service areas: Boston, Back Bay, Brookline, Lexington, and Bedford. Virtual therapy is available for clients in MA, VT, NH, and RI.

We collaborate with families and—when requested—referring professionals and school teams.

Crisis / Emergency Information

Esplanade Psychological Services is not an emergency or crisis service. If you or your child is at immediate risk of harm, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988 or use the 988 Lifeline chat. In Massachusetts, you may also call or text the Behavioral Health Help Line at 833-773-2445.
