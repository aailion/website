Therapy

Approach

I have an eclectic approach to therapy that is engaging and interactive. I have advanced training in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Mindfulness Techniques, and Dialectical Behavioral Therapy. I also have experience with Executive Functioning Training - including the Unstuck and On Target! cirriculum and cognitive remediation programs.

My theraputic approach is strength based, insight oriented skill building collaboration. I’d be happy to offer a free consultation to determine if I am the right therapist for your family. Please feel free to email or call me to set up an initial consultation.
