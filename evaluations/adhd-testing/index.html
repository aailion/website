ADHD Testing

Evaluation to clarify attention and executive functioning concerns and differentiate ADHD from other contributors.

Start here

Send a brief message and we’ll follow up by email. Please don’t include sensitive clinical details.

Request a Free 15-Minute Consultation Email EsplanadePsych@gmail.com

ADHD symptoms can overlap with anxiety, depression, learning differences, sleep issues, and medical factors. A comprehensive evaluation helps clarify what is driving attention and executive-functioning challenges and what supports are most likely to help.

What we look at

  • Attention, working memory, and processing speed
  • Executive functioning (planning, organization, inhibition, flexibility)
  • Learning and academic skills (when relevant)
  • Emotional functioning and stress/anxiety impacts
  • Developmental, medical, and school history

Outcomes

Families and providers often use results to guide:

  • School supports and accommodations (IEP/504)
  • Therapy goals and skill-building strategies
  • Medication decision-making in collaboration with medical providers

Crisis / Emergency Information

Esplanade Psychological Services is not an emergency or crisis service. If you or your child is at immediate risk of harm, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988 or use the 988 Lifeline chat. In Massachusetts, you may also call or text the Behavioral Health Help Line at 833-773-2445.
