If you’re worried about your child’s learning, attention, emotions, or day-to-day functioning, it can be hard to know where to start. Our evaluations are designed to bring clarity—so families and referral partners have a practical plan forward.

We provide in-person neuropsychological and psychoeducational evaluations for children, teens, and young adults (ages 5–25) in the Boston area. Every evaluation is tailored to the referral questions—whether concerns involve ADHD, autism, learning differences, executive functioning, emotional/behavioral symptoms, or complex and medical presentations (including neurological factors).

What families receive

  • Clinical interview and history
  • Review of relevant records (school, medical, prior testing)
  • Standardized testing selected for the referral questions
  • Feedback session to review results and recommendations
  • A comprehensive, integrated written report with diagnoses and recommendations when appropriate

If helpful, we can also collaborate with treatment teams and schools (e.g., IEP/504 planning, school consultation, or meetings) for an additional fee.

Neuropsychological Evaluation
Comprehensive assessment of cognition, learning, attention, executive functioning, and emotional/behavioral factors.
Psychoeducational Evaluation
Academic testing and learning profile analysis to guide targeted school supports and accommodations.
ADHD Testing
Evaluation to clarify attention and executive functioning concerns and differentiate ADHD from other contributors.
Autism Evaluation
Assessment of social-communication, learning, and emotional/behavioral functioning to clarify ASD and guide supports.
Learning Disability / Dyslexia Evaluation
Assessment to clarify reading, writing, math, and learning differences and guide evidence-based interventions.
Executive Functioning Evaluation & Support
Understand planning, organization, flexibility, and time-management skills—and build a practical plan.
Concussion / TBI & Cognitive Rehabilitation
Assessment and treatment planning for attention, memory, and learning changes following injury or illness.
College & Standardized Testing Accommodations
Documentation and recommendations to support accommodations for school, college, and standardized exams when appropriate.
School Consultation / IEP & 504 Support
Collaboration with schools and teams to translate results into practical supports and accommodations.
