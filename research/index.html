Research & Academic Work

Research interests in pediatric neuropsychology, epilepsy, language, neuroimaging, and outcomes.

Dr. Alyssa Ailion’s research focuses on how neurological and medical conditions interact with brain networks, cognition, language, learning, and everyday functioning in children, adolescents, and young adults. Her academic work bridges clinical neuropsychology, pediatric neurology, neuroimaging, and outcomes research.

Research interests

Areas of research interest include:

  • Pediatric neuropsychology
  • Epilepsy and epilepsy surgery outcomes
  • Neurosurgical neuropsychology
  • Language development and language mapping
  • Brain plasticity and neurodevelopment
  • Functional connectivity and neuroimaging
  • Cognitive, academic, and psychosocial outcomes
  • Measurement and methods development

Crisis / Emergency Information

Esplanade Psychological Services is not an emergency or crisis service. If you or your child is at immediate risk of harm, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988 or use the 988 Lifeline chat. In Massachusetts, you may also call or text the Behavioral Health Help Line at 833-773-2445.
