Who to refer
  • Academic underachievement or a gap between potential and performance
  • Concerns about ADHD, autism, learning differences/dyslexia, or executive functioning
  • Anxiety/mood symptoms impacting functioning
  • Medical/neurological concerns (e.g., concussion/TBI, prematurity)
  • Need for IEP/504 planning, school placement guidance, or accommodations
What families receive
  • Clinical interview + record review
  • Standardized testing tailored to referral questions
  • Feedback session
  • Comprehensive integrated written report with recommendations
  • Optional school/provider consultation
How to refer
  • Ask the family to complete the website contact form: https://yourbrain.doctor/contact/
  • Or email: EsplanadePsych@gmail.com

Please avoid sending sensitive clinical details by email.

Helpful records
  • IEP/504 plans
  • Prior evaluations
  • Report cards/transcripts
  • Standardized test history
  • Relevant medical records
  • Therapy summaries (as appropriate)
  • Teacher observations
